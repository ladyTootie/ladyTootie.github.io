## Project description
This page contains links to my documentation for the senior project I did in collaboration with NIWIC and Core4ce. I had to conduct research on the reliability of AI/Machine Learning-based Intrusion Detection Systems and create a proof of concept displaying the strengths and weaknesses of such systems. The following documentation contains sources that helped me develop a research plan, process of synthesizing my hypothesis, designing the testbed, data acquisition, and analysis of my test cases. There is also a white paper that summarizes my whole research experience and my findings. In total, there are 10 documents, and they will be listed in order of completion.
